What is a Softswitch?

The Core of Modern VoIP Networks

A Softswitch (Software Switch) is a central component in modern VoIP networks, responsible for routing calls, managing billing, and connecting IP-based and traditional telephony systems. It enables seamless communication over the internet, reducing costs while increasing flexibility and control.

Routes and manages VoIP call traffic

Connects IP and traditional telephony systems

Handles billing, CDRs, and rate management

Provides advanced call features and security
